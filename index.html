About Edward

Built on family, public service, and a record of responsibility.

Edward Burchett is a lifelong resident of Bossier Parish whose values were shaped by a strong family tradition of public service. He grew up around people who believed public office should be handled with integrity, responsibility, and respect for the community.

That foundation has influenced how he approaches leadership today: steady, accountable, and focused on doing the job the right way. He believes public trust is earned through fairness, professionalism, and consistent service.

In his professional career, Edward has built a reputation for solving problems, improving systems, and handling high-level responsibilities where accuracy matters. He has worked in demanding environments, helped strengthen operations, and been entrusted with significant responsibility, including holding a Top Secret security clearance.

He wants to bring that same seriousness, discipline, and commitment to service to the work ahead.
