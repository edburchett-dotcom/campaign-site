About Edward Burchett

Edward Burchett is a lifelong resident of Bossier Parish. He has been around public service most of his life, which helped shape how he approaches leadership—with a focus on integrity, responsibility, and doing things the right way.

Edward built his career around accountability, efficiency, and problem-solving. He has spent years working with complex systems where accuracy matters, improving operations, and helping ensure processes function fairly and effectively. He has been trusted with high-level responsibilities throughout his career, including holding a Top Secret security clearance.

He believes the Tax Assessor’s office should operate with fairness, transparency, and accountability to the people it serves.
